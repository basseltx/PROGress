HTPN PROGress

Using PROGress

  • The HTPN PROGress tool

    This manual explains how to use PROGress, the HTPN patient data entry tool.

  • Set up centricity to use PROGress

    Getting ready - Centricity set up.

    This is done by the medical assistant, nurse or other clinical staff only.

  • Previsit planning

    Setting the patient up for data entry, picking the forms needed to be filled out.

    This activity is conducted by the medical assistant, nurse, or clinical staff.

  • When the patient arrives, have patient enter their information.

    Collecting the information from the patient (parent).

    This activity may be conducted by the clinic's front desk staff, or at the point of care in the exam room by medical assistant, nurse, or clinical staff.

  • Capture the patient reported observations in Centricity

    This is where the patient entered information is pulled back into Centricity.

    This activity is conducted by the Medical Assistant, nurse, or other clinical staff.
